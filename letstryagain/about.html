ABOUT ME

My name is Milda Mockute, I am from Lithuania and live in a resort called Palanga. I must say that I am a passionate traveler, who wants to share her experience online. My goal is to travel to as many places as I can, including local cities of my own origin. I hope that you enjoyed reading my blog and will be waiting for more trip reviews and other upcoming destinations, which I would like to travel to. I would like to speak with fellow travelers and exchange information regarding your experiences.
